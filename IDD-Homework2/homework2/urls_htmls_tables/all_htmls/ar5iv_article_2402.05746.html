3ji5 W1 : (a) Clarification of innovations; (b) Detailed descriptions. (a) This work presents two significant innovations. 1) From a system perspective, our editable driving scene simulator (ChatSim) is clearly novel. Regarding functionality, ChatSim is the first to achieve editable 3D driving scene simulations via language commands. Regarding system design, this is the first time that a multi-LLM-agent collaboration framework is used for scene editing; 2) from a technical perspective, our lighting estimation method, McLight, has two novel designs: (i) a multi-camera fusion module that uses an attention operation to aggregate multi-view information at the feature level for enhanced skydome lighting prediction; and (ii) a NeRF-coupled querying approach that estimates surrounding lighting based on HDR scene radiance from exposure-aware McNeRF, for modeling of complex surrounding objects’ illumination effects. (b) For LLM-agents, see the framework introduction in Section 3.2 and examples of prompts in Common Q2. Multi-camera alignment is not technically novel but is practical and generic to significantly improve rendering quality.
