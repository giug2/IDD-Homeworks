5 Conclusion

In this work, we propose a synthetic data augmentation algorithm and prompting strategy that effectively complement each other, enabling efficient task-specific adaptation of LLMs for reasoning tasks. We plan to expand our work to include a broader range of graph-based reasoning tasks.

Limitations

Potential limitations of this work include not experimenting with larger open-source LLMs, such as the Mixtral 8x7BÂ Jiang etÂ al. (2024) or 70B LLaMa3Â AI@Meta (2024), as well as other recent models like GemmaÂ Team etÂ al. (2024). Additionally, the study did not incorporate more closed-source models such as ClaudeÂ Anthropic (2024) to further study the impact of history enhancement. This is mainly due to limitations in computational (open source) and financial (closed source) resources. While experiments show promise in enhancing LLM reasoning abilities without compromising their generalization performance, exploring more datasets and tasks can further enhance the credibility of our results.
